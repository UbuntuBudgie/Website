Start working

Coming from Mac?

We’ve developed an applet that allows you to have Hot corners function that you used while running OS X. Want to use Expose? Open specific app? No problem, just put the mouse in the corner.

Get me some weather!

Do you need to carry that Umbrella? Going out in a bit? Cloudy with chance of falling frogs? Have a quick look on your desktop and prepare for outside world.

Need better screen overview?

With shuffler applet you can arrange open apps by snaping them across the screen manually or via keyboard in any direction.

Extend your desktop experience

Small, but useful

Ubuntu Budgie comes with various applets, that extend the features of Budgie desktop. More are available from Budgie Welcome.

Drop By

Get to your files faster without the need to open Nemo to browse other disks and locations.

Auto-Switcher

Switch languages easily for each app and desktop, for those that use bilingual keyboards.

Workspaces

Control different workspaces and name each individually from the taskbar.

Quick Note

Copy & Paste or write quick notes that you want to get reminded about later.

Activities Overview

Check what is happening on other desktops, open applications, without switching desktops.

Brightness & Backlight controller

Easily control your screen brightness and keyboard backlight from your taskbar.
